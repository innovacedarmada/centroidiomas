Reading Test: Our Favorite Things

INSTRUCTIONS:

READ THE TEXT ABOUT THREE FRIENDS' FAVORITE THINGS.

NOW, DECIDE IF THE FOLLOWING STATEMENTS ARE TRUE OR FALSE.

Tom, Lisa, and Sam are good friends. They like to talk about their favorite things. Tom has a new bike. It is faster than Lisa’s old bike. Lisa has a big bag for school. It is bigger than Tom’s small bag. Sam loves animals. His cat, Leo, is friendlier than his neighbor’s dog. Of all their pets, Leo is the friendliest pet in the neighborhood!

Lisa loves reading. Her new fantasy book is more interesting than the old book. She thinks it is the most interesting book she has ever read. Tom loves his computer. He thinks his computer is the fastest one for playing games. Sam loves to travel. He says his holiday to the mountains was better than his holiday to the beach. He says it was the best holiday of his life!

True or False Statements

1. Tom's new bike is faster than Lisa's old bike.

2. Lisa’s bag is smaller than Tom’s bag.

3. Sam's cat is the friendliest animal in the neighborhood.

4. Lisa’s new book is the most interesting one she has ever read.

5. Tom’s computer is the best for playing games.

6. Sam’s holiday to the beach was better than his holiday to the mountains.

7. Lisa's old book was more interesting than her new one.

8. Sam thinks his holiday to the mountains was the best holiday of his life.

9. Tom's bag is bigger than Lisa's.

10. Sam's cat is friendlier than his neighbor's dog.
